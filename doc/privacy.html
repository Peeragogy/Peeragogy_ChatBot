Privacy Policy – PeeragogyBot

Last Updated: April 2025

Your privacy is important to us. PeeragogyBot is an experimental AI assistant designed to support collaborative and reflective learning experiences. This policy explains how we handle information during your interaction.

PeeragogyBot relies on OpenAI's language models and may interact with additional services (such as Flowise-based APIs or knowledge bases) to enhance interactions.

What Data We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data. Conversations are processed in real-time and are not retained beyond the current session. No identifying information is required or requested during use.

Use of External Services

PeeragogyBot may communicate with external tools and APIs to answer questions or reflect on content. These interactions are temporary and anonymized. We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking technologies.

Responsible Use

This chatbot is provided for educational and peeragogical experimentation only. Users are advised not to share sensitive, private, or confidential information during interactions.

About the Project

PeeragogyBot is part of the open-source Pyragogy Project, which explores human-AI co-learning. For more details or to contribute, please visit our website.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about data usage, please contact us at: info@pyragogy.org
